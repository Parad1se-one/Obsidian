# So, Tell Me About Your Policy...: Distillation of Interpretable Policies from Deep Reinforcement Learning Agents

**arXiv:** 2507.xxxxx (July 2025)  
**Authors:** Research on interpretable RL

## Summary
This paper addresses the interpretability challenge in deep reinforcement learning by distilling complex neural network policies into interpretable representations. The work enables understanding and explanation of RL agent behaviors.

## Key Contributions
- **Interpretable Policy Distillation:** Transforms black-box neural policies into interpretable formats
- **Explanation Framework:** Provides human-understandable explanations of agent decisions
- **Behavior Preservation:** Maintains performance while improving interpretability

## Method
- Teacher: Complex deep RL policy (neural network)
- Student: Interpretable policy representation (decision trees, rules, or symbolic policies)
- Distillation process preserves key behaviors while enabling interpretation
- Allows querying and explanation of policy decisions

## Applications
- Safety-critical RL applications
- Healthcare and autonomous systems
- Regulatory compliance
- Debugging and analyzing RL agents
- Human-AI collaboration

- Addresses the "black box" problem in deep RL
