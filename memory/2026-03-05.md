### New Recommended Direction: Efficient World Models

**Core idea:** Don't pursue large-scale pretraining; instead, reduce compute costs of existing world models.

**Specific techniques:**
1. Knowledge distillation (large model → small model)
2. Model compression (pruning, quantization)
3. Sparse activation (MoE, sparse networks)
4. Early exiting (shallow networks for simple states)

**Proposed title:**
"LightDreamer: Efficient World Models via Knowledge Distillation and Sparse Activation"

**Expected contributions:**
